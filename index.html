About Me

I’m Swati Kumari, a UI/UX designer with a foundation in architecture. My journey from designing physical spaces to crafting digital experiences has given me a unique perspective on balancing aesthetics, functionality, and usability. I specialize in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and design systems, ensuring every project I take on is both intuitive and impactful.

I believe design goes beyond visuals—it’s about creating seamless experiences that make technology feel natural. With strong collaboration, adaptability, and empathy, I’ve worked on projects ranging from e-commerce to health-tech, always focusing on solving real problems for real people. Outside of work, I enjoy sketching, exploring design trends, and learning about human interaction with technology, which continually inspires my creativity.
